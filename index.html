Bangladesh is a country in South Asia. It is the seventh-largest country in the world by area, and the second-most populous country. Bangladesh is bordered by India to the north, and Burma to the south. It shares land borders with Myanmar and Bangladesh. The country is divided into two main administrative divisions: the Dhaka Division and the Chittagong Division. The former is the capital and largest city, while the latter is the second-largest city. The country's total area is about 142,817 square kilometres (51,972 sq mi), and its population is about 18,764,000.
